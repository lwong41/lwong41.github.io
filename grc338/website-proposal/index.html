Introduction

Stardust Cosmetics

Stardust Cosmetics is based in San Francisco, CA and prides itself on being cruelty free and sustainable. Additionally, we have an on-going program that focuses on donating some of the proceeds to various charities around the world. Who says you can't be beautiful while also helping the earth and your community!

Target audience

The target audience is young adult females and males that are interested in a cosmetic company that is cruelty free, sustainable, and dedicated to giving back to the community. Stardust Cosmetics doesn't test any products on animals which ensures that they only carry cruelty free products. Additionally, they make products in reusable containers that can be refilled in shop. And lastly, once a month, Stardust Cosmetics chooses a weekend where 10% of every sale gets donated to a random charity.

Stardust Cosmetics goals include providing cosmetics to consumers that is made with safe ingredients and is made cruelty free (mainly focusing on having no animal testing associated with the product), creating a sustainable brand to help decrease the amount of waste produced daily, and to give back to the community in whatever way possible. Stardust Cosmetics wants its customers to feel proud to use their cosmetics and to feel like they are making a different while looking beautiful.

About Us

Stardust Cosmetics

All Stardust Cosmetics are created cruelty free and focus on sustainability and giving back.

At Stardust Cosmetics we pride ourselves on giving our customers the very best. Our products are made cruelty free ensuring that no animals are harmed during the process.
Additionally we are a sustainable brand. We focus mostly on providing sustainable packaging, meaning our packaging is reusable! Simply keep the container once it's empty and bring it back to your nearest location. The store will have an in-house refill station for you to refill your bottles at a slightly discounted price!
Lastly, we at Stardust Cosmetics want to give back to the community. To do this, we choose one weekend every month where 10% of all sales get donated to a random charity. The charity is chosen by our customers through a survey and we intend on keeping this as a monthly tradition!
